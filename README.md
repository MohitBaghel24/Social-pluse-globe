## вңҰ What is Social Pulse Globe?

Social Pulse Globe is a **single-file, zero-dependency** intelligence dashboard that renders a 3D Earth and visualises which social media platform dominates each country вҖ” updated live. Click any country to explore platform breakdowns, trending topics, AI-generated insights, and regional buzz.

Think: *"What if every country's internet had a live heartbeat вҖ” and you could see it rotating in space."*

## вҡЎ Features

### рҹҢҗ Interactive 3D Globe
- Fully rotatable globe rendered with **Globe.gl + Three.js**
- Country heatmaps coloured by dominant social platform
- Click any country вҶ’ instant stats panel with flag, platform breakdown, trends
- Auto-rotation when idle В· Momentum-based drag controls
- Geolocation detection вҖ” highlights your country on load

### вҳ  WAR ROOM вҖ” Platform Battle Mode
- Real-time territory dominance scoreboard
- Fighter HP bars for every platform (WhatsApp, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, WeChat, X)
- Live Territory Feed вҖ” shows which platform just "seized" which country
- AI-powered Next Battle Prediction with probability scores

### рҹ“Ҡ Country Intelligence Panel
- Platform usage breakdown bar chart per country
- Trending topics (localised per country)
- AI Insight card with regional social media analysis
- Platform Buzz tab with post activity data

### рҹ“Ў Live Ticker + Stats
- Bottom scrolling news ticker вҖ” 14 global social media headlines
- Live posts/sec counter (updates every 750ms)
- 195 countries tracked

### рҹҺҜ Platform Switcher
- 9 platforms: All В· Facebook В· YouTube В· WhatsApp В· Instagram В· TikTok В· WeChat В· X В· Snapchat
- Globe recolours instantly on switch
- Kill Feed overlay (COD-style) вҖ” live platform dominance alerts

### в¬Ў Viral Tracker
- Visual arc map of how viral content migrated between countries (2020вҖ“2026)
